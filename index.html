Giving Tuesday in 2018: November 27th! Get ready to give!



What is Giving Tuesday??

Giving Tuesday is a day around the Thanksgiving season to give back, contrary to the popular days of Black Friday and Cyber Monday, which are centered on oneself. It is the day after Cyber Monday, and has gained most of its popularity on social media. The day is currently in its sixth year of official existence.

How Do I Get Involved??

Most people get involved by donating money to their online website, but many people get involved by helping around their local comunities. They often donate their time by doing volunteer work, donating food or other necessities, or help with nonprofits and campaigns.

Where should I go to contribute?

You can pretty much go anywhere to help, just be nice to people and GIVE! If you want to be a part of the larger Giving Tuesday community, however, click here ! Share what YOU give!
